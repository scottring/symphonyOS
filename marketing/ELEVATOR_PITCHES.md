## Objection Handling

**"We already use [tool]—why switch?"**
*What's it costing you to search for information? How many times this week did someone ask "what's for dinner?" or "when do I need to be there?" Symphony gives you back that time.*

**"Sounds complicated to set up."**
*5-minute onboarding walks you through. You'll have your first week planned in less time than your Sunday evening scroll through Instagram.*

**"My partner won't use another app."**
*They don't have to plan—they can just participate. See their tasks, get reminders, mark things done. Or use Symphony's read-only view for family visibility.*

**"We're fine with pen and paper/whiteboard."**
*Love the intentionality of analog! Symphony is the digital version of that weekly planning ritual—but it comes with you on your phone and reminds you when it's time.*
