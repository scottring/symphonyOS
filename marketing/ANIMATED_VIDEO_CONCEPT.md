# Symphony OS: 60-Second Animated Video Concept

*Target: Social media (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn), landing page hero*
*Duration: 60 seconds*
*Style: Warm, minimalist 2D animation with subtle motion*
*Color palette: Scandinavian neutrals with forest green accents*

---

## Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:05)
*5 seconds*

### Visual
- Chaotic scene: Multiple app icons spinning in space
- Calendar notifications popping up
- Text messages flying by
- Post-it notes fluttering
- Everything in harsh, overwhelming colors
- Speed and chaos increasing

### Audio
- Discordant notification sounds layering
- Subtle stress-inducing heartbeat rhythm underneath

### Text Overlay
**"Managing family life is chaos."**

### Transition
- Everything suddenly STOPS mid-spin
- Apps/notifications freeze in air
- Hold for 1 beat
- Then everything gently dissolves into particles
- Particles settle into calm, organized constellation

## Feature 1: Context When You Need It (0:15 - 0:25)
*10 seconds*

### Visual
**Scene:** Today view with animated task card

- Task card titled "Call dentist" appears
- Card expands to reveal detail panel
- Contents cascade in smoothly:
  - Phone number with tap-to-call icon
  - Notes section with bullet points appearing
  - Linked document icon
  - Contact photo
- All elements connected by subtle dotted lines
- Green checkmark appears after brief pause

- Soft shadows grow as panel expands

- Smooth spring physics for all movements

### Audio
- Soft "whoosh" as panel opens

- Satisfying "click" for the checkmark

### Voiceover
*"Everything you need, right when you need it. No searching."*

## Feature 2: Timeline Companion (0:25 - 0:38)
*13 seconds - The hero moment*

### Visual
**Scene:** Subway-map style Timeline Companion

- Three horizontal lines representing family members
- Each line labeled with simple avatar + name
- Colored dots begin moving along the lines from left to right
- Lines converge at shared moments:
  - Breakfast (all three meet)
  - Dinner (all three meet)
- Lines diverge for individual activities:
  - School (kid's line goes to school building)
  - Work (parent's line goes to office)
  - Meeting (other parent's line stays home)
- Time markers flow past like a train window
- Conflict point highlighted: Two events overlapping
  - Gentle yellow glow around conflict
  - Alternative paths suggested with dotted lines

### Animation Details
- Smooth, meditative dot movement
- 3-second journey along timeline
- Convergence points pulse gently
- Conflict detection feels helpful, not alarming
- Subtle particle effects at convergence points
- Overall feeling: Calm, controlled, beautiful

### Audio
- Gentle rhythmic sound as dots move (like train tracks)
- Harmonious chord when family members converge
- Soft alert tone (not jarring) at conflict point
- Resolution sound as alternative appears

### Voiceover
*"See your family's day like a subway map. Know where everyone is, spot conflicts before they happen."*

### Key Moment
- This is the "wow" shot - hold it longest
- Most visually distinctive element
- Should be instantly recognizable/shareable

## Technical Production Notes

### Style Guide

**Color Palette:**
```
Background: hsl(32 20% 95%) - Warm cream
Primary: hsl(157 31% 40%) - Forest green
Accents: hsl(32 40% 85%) - Warm neutral
Text: hsl(32 10% 20%) - Dark brown-black
```

**Typography:**
- Display: Fraunces (serif) for elegance
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) for clarity
- Weights: 400, 600, 800

**Animation Principles:**
1. Ease-in-out for all transitions (no linear)
2. Spring physics for UI elements
3. Stagger effects for multiple elements (80-100ms delays)
4. Everything should feel organic, not robotic
5. Generous use of whitespace - never cramped
6. Subtle motion - nothing jarring or hyperactive

**Illustration Style:**
- 2D flat design with subtle depth (soft shadows)
- Rounded corners on everything (12-16px radius)
- Minimalist icons, high recognition
- Human silhouettes (abstract, diverse)
- Warm, inviting, Scandinavian aesthetic
- Not corporate - friendly and approachable

---

## Voiceover Direction

**Voice Profile:**
- Gender: Neutral to warm female voice
- Age: 30-45 sound
- Accent: Neutral American (slight warmth, not clinical)
- Tone: Confident but not corporate
- Pacing: Measured, clear, not rushed
- Emotion: Empathetic, knowing, reassuring

**Specific Direction:**
- Opening line: Acknowledge the pain, validate the struggle
- Product intro: Confident, clear, almost revelatory
- Feature descriptions: Warm, helpful, conversational
- Timeline segment: Let the visual lead, voice is supportive
- Emotional payoff: Genuine warmth, not schmaltzy
- CTA: Inviting, not pushy

**Avoid:**
- Overly enthusiastic startup energy
- Corporate robot voice
- Condescending "helpful" tone
- Rushed/urgent delivery
- Fake excitement

---

## Audio/Music Direction

**Music Composition:**
- Minimal, modern, acoustic instruments
- Lead: Warm piano or kalimba
- Texture: Ambient pads, subtle strings
- NO drums/beats - keep it organic
- Build gradually from opening to emotional payoff
- Resolve cleanly at end

**Sound Design:**
- UI sounds: Soft, tactile, satisfying
- No harsh beeps or alerts
- Natural soundscapes (coffee shop, home ambience)
- Everything supports the calm aesthetic
- Reference: Things 3, Headspace app sounds

**Mix:**
- Music: 30% volume
- Voiceover: 60% volume (clear but not dominating)
- Sound effects: 10% (subtle supporting role)
- Overall: Spacious, not crowded
- Dynamics: Gentle, no sudden changes

## Versions & Variants

### 30-Second Cut (Social Media)
- Keep: Opening hook, Timeline Companion, Emotional payoff, CTA
- Remove: Context reveal, AI meal planning, Desktop/Mobile split
- Focus: The hero visual (Timeline) + emotional hook

### 15-Second Teaser
- Keep: Opening hook (3 sec) + Timeline visual (8 sec) + CTA (4 sec)
- Pure visual impact, minimal voice
- "Less chaos. More life. Symphony OS."

### Square Format (Instagram/Facebook Feed)
- Reframe all scenes for 1:1 ratio
- Larger text/UI elements
- Same narrative flow, adjusted compositions

### Vertical Format (Stories/Reels/TikTok)
- 9:16 ratio
- UI elements larger and centered
- Timeline orientation stays horizontal (letterbox effect is OK here)
- More dramatic vertical text reveals

## Closing Notes

**What Makes This Work:**

1. **Leads with pain, not features** - The chaos opening is instantly relatable
2. **Hero visual is unique** - Timeline Companion is unlike anything else
3. **Shows, doesn't tell** - Every feature is demonstrated, not described
4. **Emotional arc** - Chaos → Solution → Relief
5. **Calm aesthetic matches product** - The video feels like using the app
6. **Clear CTA** - One action, obvious path

**What to Avoid:**

- Generic productivity platitudes
- Cheesy stock footage families
- Over-explaining features
- Rushed pacing
- Corporate stock music
- Fake enthusiasm

**The Goal:**

Make someone watching on their phone during a chaotic morning go: *"Wait, what is that? I need that."*

Then make it easy for them to sign up.
