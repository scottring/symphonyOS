## Technical Production Notes

### Style Guide

**Color Palette:**
```
Background: hsl(32 20% 95%) - Warm cream
Primary: hsl(157 31% 40%) - Forest green
Accents: hsl(32 40% 85%) - Warm neutral
Text: hsl(32 10% 20%) - Dark brown-black
Conflict: hsl(45 90% 60%) - Soft yellow (not alarming)
```

**Typography:**
- Display: Fraunces (serif) for elegance
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) for clarity
- Weights: 400, 600, 800

**Animation Principles:**
1. Ease-in-out for all transitions (no linear)
2. Spring physics for UI elements (feels organic)
3. Stagger effects for multiple elements (80-100ms delays)
4. Nothing jarring or hyperactive
5. Generous whitespace - breathing room
6. Subtle motion - calm, not frenetic

**Illustration Style:**
- 2D flat design with subtle depth (soft shadows)
- Rounded corners on everything (12-16px radius)
- Minimalist icons, high recognition
- Human silhouettes (abstract, inclusive)
- Warm, inviting, Scandinavian aesthetic
- Not corporate - friendly and capable

## Voiceover Direction

**Voice Profile:**
- Gender: Neutral to warm female voice
- Age: 30-45 sound
- Accent: Neutral American (slight warmth)
- Tone: Confident but not corporate, capable but not pushy
- Pacing: Measured, clear, not rushed
- Emotion: Understanding, reassuring, honest

**Specific Direction:**
- Opening: Acknowledge the frustration (empathetic)
- Product intro: Confident, clear (this is the solution)
- Feature descriptions: Warm, helpful (here's how it works)
- Beta CTA: Inviting, not pushy (join us)
- Timeline teaser: Exciting but honest (coming soon)

**Full Script:**
```
"You have the information somewhere...

Symphony OS. Your family's command center.

Everything you need, right when you need it. No searching.

Plan your week. Spot conflicts. Fix them before they're chaos.

Everyone knows who's doing what. Fair, visible, clear.

Plan deeply. Execute quickly. One system.

Join the beta.

Timeline Companion: Coming soon. Preview in beta."
```

**Avoid:**
- Overly enthusiastic startup energy
- Corporate robot voice
- Condescending "helpful" tone
- Rushed delivery
- Fake excitement

## Audio/Music Direction

**Music Composition:**
- Minimal, modern, acoustic
- Lead: Warm piano or kalimba
- Texture: Ambient pads, subtle strings
- NO drums/beats - keep it organic
- Build gradually from stress to calm
- Resolve cleanly at end

**Sound Design:**
- UI sounds: Soft, tactile, satisfying
- No harsh beeps or alerts
- Natural soundscapes (coffee shop, home)
- Everything supports the calm aesthetic
- Reference: Things 3, Headspace sounds

**Mix:**
- Music: 30% volume (supportive, not dominant)
- Voiceover: 60% volume (clear but natural)
- Sound effects: 10% (subtle accents)
- Overall: Spacious, not crowded
- Dynamics: Gentle, no sudden changes
